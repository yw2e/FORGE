Dennis Fong (CEO)

Dennis stands as a renowned figure, recognized as the inaugural "professional gamer" to clinch championships in games like Doom and Quake. His journey underscores how gaming can propel individuals to remarkable achievements and groundbreaking careers. Additionally, he's the architect behind gaming social startups such as Xfire, Raptr, and http://plays.tv, all of which have left an indelible mark on the gaming community.

Kun Gao (COO)

A prominent figure among anime enthusiasts, Kun Gao has established several startups, including @Crunchyroll, a streaming platform that revolutionized the anime-watching experience. His endeavors exemplify the convergence of gaming and entertainment, illustrating the dynamic nature of this industry.



George Ng (CTO)

The mastermind behind Forge's technological framework, George has a history of co-founding and serving as CTO of Cyance. His extensive expertise in devising robust tech solutions is evident, underscoring the critical role of cutting-edge technology in realizing Forge's vision.
